Protocol Description	Arabidopsis Col-0 and homozygous UGT74E2OE3.10 seeds were sown on half-strength MS plant medium (2.15 g/L MS basal medium (Duchefa, http://www.duchefa.com/), 0.5 g/L 2-(N-morpholino)ethanesulfonic acid, 0.1 g/L myo-inositol, 10 g/L sucrose, 7 g/L plant tissue culture agar (LabM, http://www.labm.com/), 0.5 mg/L nicotinic acid, 0.5 mg/L pyridoxine and 1 mg/L thiamine) at 21C and 65 uE m-2 sec-1 radiation in a 16-h light/8-h dark photoperiod in triplicate. Per triplicate, the plantlets were harvested at developmental stage 1.04 (Boyes et al., 2001). Total RNA was isolated with TRIzol reagent (Invitrogen). Each triplicate of Arabidopsis Col-0 and homozygous transgenic plants was hybridized to one Affymetrix chip (GeneChip, Arabidopsis ATH1 Genome Array; Affymetrix, http://www.affymetrix.com).
