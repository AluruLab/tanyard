Experiment Description	Inducible overexpression of STM by AlcR / AlcA system. Plants harboring 35S::AlcR/AlcA::GOI (GUS control, STM and STM-VP16)constructs were grown in continous light for 12 days and induced with 1% Ethanol. After 12h of EtOH treatment, seedlings were dissected and RNA was processed from the shoot apex and young leaves. Affymetrix Ath1 arrays were hybridized in duplicates from each experiment.
